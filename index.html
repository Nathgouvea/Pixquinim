Our Favorite Recipes

Before After

Brazilian Stroganoff 🇧🇷

⏰ 30 mins 👥 4 servings ⭐ Favorite

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 100g mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mustard

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into strips
  2. Fry until golden brown
  3. Add mushrooms and cook
  4. Add cream, ketchup, and mustard
  5. Simmer until thickened

💡 Nathielle's tip: Serve with potato sticks on top for extra crunch!

Before After

Dutch Stamppot 🇳🇱

⏰ 45 mins 👥 4 servings 🥶 Winter favorite

Ingredients

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 500g kale or endive
  • 200g bacon
  • 1 smoked sausage
  • Butter and milk

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes until tender
  2. Cook kale separately
  3. Fry bacon until crispy
  4. Mash potatoes with milk and butter
  5. Mix in kale and bacon

💡 Jamal's tip: Don't forget the gravy!
